Panda

Aalso called giant pandas, are mammal animals that stand out, mainly, for their unmistakable white fur with black spots on their eyes and limbs, and their black ears and shoulders. These animals, despite being classified in the order Carnivore , do not have this type of food, and their diet is based on the consumption of bamboo .

Diet

Os pandas alimentam-se de grande quantidade dessa planta para conseguir manter suas necessidades nutricionais. Estima-se que eles podem alimentar-se de quase 40 kg de bambu diariamente.

How long lives?

Em cativeiro um panda pode sobreviver mais de 30 anos.

Main Habits:

  • Pandas can spend up to 14 hours a day feeding.
  • Pandas can defecate more than 100 times in a single day.
